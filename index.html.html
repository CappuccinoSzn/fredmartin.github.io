FAQs

Answers to frequently asked questions about my services and process.

What services do you offer?

I offer a range of services including graphic design (logo design, branding, marketing materials), web development (responsive websites, e-commerce solutions), and system development (custom software, database management).

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. A simple logo design might take 1-2 weeks, while a comprehensive website could take 4-8 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What is your design process?

My design process includes: 1) Discovery - understanding your needs and goals, 2) Research - exploring ideas and competitors, 3) Concept Development - creating initial designs, 4) Refinement - making adjustments based on feedback, and 5) Finalization - delivering the completed project.

Do you offer maintenance services?

Yes, I offer maintenance packages for websites and systems to ensure they remain up-to-date, secure, and functioning optimally. We can discuss maintenance options based on your specific needs.
